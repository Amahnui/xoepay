Best Industry standard anti-fraud protection technology.

XoeRemit uses machine learning and natural language processing techniques to detect fraudulent transfers. Models have been trained with millions of data samples taken over the past decade to ensure a high accuracy rate.

Xoepay constantly updates and share fraudstar details with its partner organizations to ensure that switching between providers becomes impossible. Once a fraudulent transaction is reported at any partner agency, XoeRemit leverages it platform to propagate this information to other providers.
