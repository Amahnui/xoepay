Frequently Asked Questions

What is Xoepay Payment Link?

Xoepay Payment Links are an easy way to receive payments for goods and services. You enter in the name of the customer and the amount due and send the generated link to the person who needs to pay you.

What can I do with a Xoepay Payment Link?

Xeopay Payment Links is a faster way to collect money into your bank account from your customers. On clicking the link, the customer can choose from the payment modes and complete payment accordingly. On payment completion, you will receive the amount in your bank account

How can I send a Xeopay Payment Link?

You can send a Xeopay Payment Link to your customer via email, SMS, Whatsapp and Facebook Messenger.

How secure is XoePay?

Protecting the data you trust to XoePay is our first priority. This part is really crucial in keeping the project in line to completion.

Can I send Payment Links in bulk?

Yes, you can send Payment Links in bulk with the Batch Uploads feature. You can upload a CSV file containing the list of customers, amount requested and their contact details and send the requests in bulk.

What are the advantages of a Payment Link

There are clear advantages to this method. You don’t need to rent the card processing machine and you don’t need to have a website. Customers don’t need to have a special account anywhere, they can just use their preferred payment option through the payment link
