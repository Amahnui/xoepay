Security and compliance at the core

We take data security as the first priority in the the way we design and deliver systems at Xoepay. We have industry level experts and we are in partnership with world class firms to make sure we implement security at its best.

  • All card numbers are encrypted on disk with AES-256. Decryption keys are stored on separate machines.

  • Our infrastructure for storing, decrypting, and transmitting card numbers runs in separate hosting infrastructure, and doesn’t share any credentials with Xoepay’s primary services.

  • We have the highest level of security compliance and we have obtained all regulatory license from our area of service. We're PCI DSS level 1 certified.
